We're bringing the I/O experience to Kuala Lumpur!

The Google I/O Extended Kuala Lumpur is a full day event of workshops and talks centered around the technologies announced during Google I/O 2015. You will be joined by a team of Google Developer Advocates and Google Developer Experts talking and sharing with you insights and technical details on the latest Android M, Android development, Polymer, Virtual Reality and many more!
